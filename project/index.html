About Department

            The Department was started in the year 1993 & propogates the light of education & knowledge amongst the students of remate regions of Tirunelveli who want to pursue education by overcoming the hurdles of their day to day life.
            The Department has competent faculty imbued with a nobel mission of producing disciplined, competent, socially commited and morally upright intellectuals through education to young and promising students for their empowerment and growth.
